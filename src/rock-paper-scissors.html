Game Instructions

Play the classic Rock, Paper, Scissors game against the computer.

Choose your move by clicking one of the images move. The computer will randomly select its move, and the winner will be determined based on the rules of the game.

You can play by using your keyboard: press R for Rock, P for Paper, and S for Scissors.

You can also enable Auto Play to let the computer play against itself.
